Simple Paint Program(C++ & OpenGL/GLUT)

In this assignment, I built a simple painting program(like a super light version of MS Paint). I used GLUT to implement a drop down menu that is attacked to the right click button of the mouse so that a use can pick a color and a shape to paint on the canvas. The menu also provide functions of picking random color and clearing the canvas.
As a special requirement, we are required to not use built-in primitive types so I used Bresenham’s Circle Algorithm for line and circle drawing and used line to achieve rectangle drawing. As a drawback of using self-built-in algorithm, the speed of drawing is very slow and inefficient.



Terrain Modeling(C++ & OpenGL/GLUT)

In this assignment, I built an interactive terrain mesh with C++ and OpenGL/GLUT. In this Terrain Modeling program, a user can add terrian ramdomly and the terrian are demonstrated with improved color mapping(topographic maps). The lowest parts of mountains are in green, higher parts are in orange and the highest are in gray. Keyboard keys 'W' and 'L' are pressed to toggle the wiregram and lighting affects correspondingly.
